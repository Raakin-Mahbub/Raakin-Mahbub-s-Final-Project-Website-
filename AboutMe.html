About me

Hello there, I am an illustrator based in New York and student currently attending Queens College; majoring in Design with a concentration in illustration. My design philosophy is that design should convey emotions all the while being pleasing for the eyes. I am interested in pursuing design as a career because I love the challenge of coming up with new ideas as well as creating something that someone can see as beautiful. For any questions or inquiries feel free to contact me.
